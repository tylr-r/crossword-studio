Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

1. Introduction

Crossword Studio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our crossword puzzle creation and playing application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for email/password authentication)
  • Google account information (if using Google Sign-In)
  • Authentication credentials managed by Firebase Authentication

2.2 User-Generated Content

We store the following data you create:

  • Custom word lists and clues you upload or create
  • Saved crossword puzzles in your cloud library
  • AI-generated content based on your prompts

2.3 Usage Data

We may collect basic usage information including:

  • Device and browser type
  • Features accessed within the application

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain user accounts
  • Store and retrieve your saved word lists and puzzles
  • Generate AI-powered content when requested
  • Improve application functionality and user experience

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and cloud storage (Firestore)
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered word list generation (optional feature)

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase's infrastructure. We implement security rules to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of using optional features like AI generation

7. Data Retention

We retain your account information and saved content until you delete your account. You may delete individual word lists at any time through the application.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
